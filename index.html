What are Pawpaws?

The pawpaw (Asimina triloba) is North America's largest native edible fruit, yet it remains one of our best-kept secrets! With a tropical flavor often described as a cross between banana and mango, pawpaws have a creamy, custard-like texture that makes them perfect for desserts.

Native to the eastern United States, pawpaws grow wild throughout Arkansas's forests. These nutritious fruits are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Join us to discover why pawpaws deserve a place in your garden and on your plate!
