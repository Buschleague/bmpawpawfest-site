Q: Is there an entry fee?

A: Nope! Admission is free and open to all ages.

Q: Can I bring my own food and drinks?

A: Yes! Coolers are welcome. Just no glass containers, please.

Q: Are pets allowed?

A: Friendly, leashed dogs are welcome. Be sure to clean up after them.

Q: Is seating provided?

A: Feel free to bring camp chairs or a picnic blanket. We'll have some seating, but it's limited.

Q: What if it rains?

Q: Will there be food and vendors?

A: Yes! Local food trucks, craft vendors, and more will be on site.

Q: Is there parking?

A: Yes! Both free and paid parking options will be available. Look for signs and volunteers directing traffic.

Q: Are there accessible restrooms?

A: Yes! An ADA-compliant porta potty will be available across from the Beard and Lady Inn.
